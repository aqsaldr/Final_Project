PART 3: PEKYS STORE EXCLUSIVE INNOVATIVE MATERIALS  
 
AEROCHILL   — ANTI-HEAT FABRIC TECHNOLOGY  

What is Aerochill?  
Aerochill is an exclusive innovative material technology from Pekys Store specifically designed to provide a cool and comfortable sensation in hot weather or during sweat-inducing activities.  

How Aerochill works:  
Aerochill fabric uses specially structured microfibers that have three main abilities:  
* 1. Moisture Wicking: absorbs and channels sweat quickly from the skin's surface to the outer layer of the fabric, keeping the skin dry  
* 2. Quick Dry: absorbed liquids evaporate faster compared to regular cotton fabrics, keeping the body cool  
* 3. Anti Odor: the fabric fibers have pores that prevent the growth of body odor-causing bacteria  

Sensation felt:  
When first wearing Aerochill, the skin will feel a light, cooling sensation like a gentle breeze.   This effect continues to be felt, especially when moving, because the air circulation inside the fabric is very good.  

Differences with regular fabrics:  
* Regular cotton t-shirt: absorbs sweat but is slow to dry, can feel sticky  
* Aerochill: absorbs sweat and immediately channels it out, skin stays dry  

Products using Aerochill: Aerochill T-Shirt, Aerochill Short Sleeve Shirt  

Suitable for:  
* Outdoor activities in hot weather  
* Light sports and walking  
* Daily wear in Indonesia's tropical climate  
* Users who sweat easily  

Safe for sensitive skin: Yes, Aerochill fibers have been tested and contain no harmful chemicals, making it safe for sensitive skin.  
Premium price: Aerochill products are sold slightly more expensive than standard products due to the more complex innovative fabric production process.  



THERMOWARM — ANTI-COLD FABRIC TECHNOLOGY  

What is ThermoWarm?  
ThermoWarm is an exclusive innovative material technology from Pekys Store designed to maintain body warmth in cold weather without having to use thick and heavy clothing.  

How ThermoWarm works:  
ThermoWarm fabric uses thermo-regulating fiber technology that works through three mechanisms:  
* 1. Heat Retention: the fabric fibers capture and store body heat, then reflect it back to the skin so the body stays warm  
* 2. Moisture Management: still capable of absorbing moisture so it doesn't make the body damp even when warm  
* 3. Lightweight Warmth: provides warmth equivalent to a thick jacket but within a thin and lightweight fabric layer  

Sensation felt:  
ThermoWarm provides even warmth across the entire body surface covered by the fabric, like a consistent warm hug.   Unlike thick jackets that are only warm in certain spots, ThermoWarm maintains even warmth.  

Warmth comparison:  
* ThermoWarm is equivalent to 1-2 layers of regular clothing at temperatures of 15-20 degrees Celsius  
* Highly suitable for air-conditioned rooms, air travel, mountainous areas  
* For extreme temperatures below 10 degrees Celsius, it is recommended to add an outer layer  

Products using ThermoWarm:  
ThermoWarm Hoodie, ThermoWarm Sweater, ThermoWarm Jacket  

Suitable for:  
* Users who are often in air-conditioned rooms  
* Travel to mountainous or highland areas  
* Winter or unpredictable weather  
* Users who get cold easily  

Aerochill vs ThermoWarm differences:  
* Aerochill: for HOT weather, keeps the body cool and dry  
* ThermoWarm: for COLD weather, keeps the body warm  
* Both are different technologies for opposite climate needs  
* It is not recommended   to wear both at the same time as their purposes are opposite

PART 5: CLOTHING CARE GUIDE  
 
STANDARD PRODUCT CARE (T-SHIRTS, SHIRTS, HOODIES, REGULAR SWEATERS)  

Washing:  
* Can be machine washed using the gentle or delicate cycle  
* Maximum water temperature is 30 degrees Celsius (lukewarm water, not hot)  
* Separate dark and light-colored clothes when washing  
* Use mild liquid detergent, avoid harsh powder detergents  
* DO NOT use bleach as it can damage fibers and fade colors  

Drying:  
* Dry in a shaded area or a well-ventilated room, avoid excessive direct sunlight as it can fade colors  
* Turn clothes inside out when drying to maintain the color on the outside  

Ironing:  
* Can be ironed on low to medium heat  
* Iron   from the inside out to protect motifs and colors  
* Fleece hoodies and sweaters do not need to be ironed, simply tidy them up while still warm after washing  

Machine dryer:  
* Not recommended to use a machine dryer as high heat can damage fiber elasticity and cause shrinkage  

Storage:  
* Store in a dry condition, folded neatly or hung  
* Avoid storing in damp conditions as it can cause a musty smell  


AEROCHILL PRODUCT CARE  

Washing:  
* Can be machine washed on gentle cycle  
* MAXIMUM water temperature is 30 degrees Celsius; hot water damages Aerochill microfibers  
* Use mild liquid detergent  
* DO NOT use fabric softener as it can clog the fiber pores that function   for air circulation and moisture wicking  
* DO NOT use bleach  

Drying:  
* Dry in a shaded area or air dry, AVOID direct sunlight  
* Aerochill is quick dry, so it will dry quickly even without direct sunlight  

Ironing:  
* NOT recommended to iron Aerochill products on high heat  
* If it needs to be neatened, use the lowest iron setting and cover with a thin cloth  

Machine dryer: NOT recommended  

Important Aerochill note:  
Avoiding fabric softener is the most crucial thing to maintain Aerochill's function.   Softener coats the fabric fibers and significantly reduces moisture wicking capabilities.  



THERMOWARM PRODUCT CARE  

Washing:  
* Can be machine washed on gentle cycle or hand wash  
* MAXIMUM water temperature is 30 degrees Celsius  
* Use mild detergent for sensitive clothes  
* DO NOT use bleach or aggressive softeners  
* Turn inside out before washing  

Drying:  
* Dry flat if possible to maintain shape, especially for ThermoWarm sweaters so they don't stretch  
* If hung, use hangers that don't leave marks on the shoulders  

Ironing:  
* NOT recommended to iron ThermoWarm products directly  
* If necessary, use the lowest temperature setting with a covering cloth  

Machine dryer: NOT recommended, heat can damage thermal fiber technology  

Dry Clean: Dry cleaning is allowed for ThermoWarm hoodies and jackets if needed

PART 7: EXCHANGE AND RETURN POLICIES  
 
PRODUCT EXCHANGE POLICY  

Pekys Store accepts product exchanges with the following conditions:  

Conditions for products that can be exchanged:  
* The product has not been worn, washed, or altered in any condition  
* Product labels and tags are still fully attached  
* The product is returned in its original packaging (original plastic wrapping)  
* Exchanges are made within 7 calendar days after the product is received  

Accepted reasons for exchange:  
* Incorrect size (too big or too small)  
* Color does not match what was   ordered (store's mistake)  
* Production defect (unraveled stitching, holes, etc.)  

Reasons not accepted:  
* The product has been worn or washed  
* Disliking the product after receiving it (not due to defects)  
* Damage caused by the customer's mistake in usage or care  

Exchange procedure:  
1. Contact Pekys Store CS via WhatsApp or Instagram DM within 7 days after the product is received  
2. Include a photo of the product and proof of purchase (order number)  
3. CS will verify and provide confirmation within 1x24 hours  
4. Once approved, send the product to the Pekys Store warehouse address provided by CS  
5. After the product is received and checked (1-2 business days), the replacement product will be sent  

Exchange shipping costs:  
* If the exchange is due to a Pekys Store mistake   (defective product, wrong color/size sent): return shipping and replacement shipping are covered by Pekys Store  
* If the exchange is due to incorrect sizing (customer's mistake in selecting size): return shipping is covered by the customer, replacement shipping is covered by Pekys Store  


REFUND POLICY  

Money refunds are issued under the following conditions:  
* The ordered product is out of stock and no replacement is approved by the customer  
* The product is defective and no replacement stock is available  
* Double order errors from the system  

Refund process:  
* Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after confirmation is approved  
* Refunds are done via bank transfer or marketplace balance according to the original payment method  

Sale products:  
Products bought during a promo sale or flash sale cannot be   exchanged or refunded unless there is a production defect.

MOST FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS  

Is there a physical Pekys Store?  
Currently, Pekys Store operates online via Shopee, Tokopedia, and Instagram. There are no physical stores yet.  

Are Big Sizes available?  
Yes. Pekys Store offers sizes up to XXXL for t-shirts, hoodies, and sweaters.   Pants are available up to size 40 (waist circumference 101-106 cm).  

Are Pekys products suitable for sports?  
Aerochill t-shirts are very suitable for light sports such as walking, yoga, and the gym due to their moisture-wicking technology.   For heavy sports, ensure you choose the appropriate products.  

Is there a size difference for men and women?  
Pekys Store uses the same size chart for men and women.   However, in general, women can choose 1 size smaller than their usual size for a more fitted look, or the same size for an oversize look.  

How long is the production time for custom orders?  
Pekys Store accepts custom orders for wholesale purchases. Production time is 14-21 business days depending on the order quantity. Contact CS for details.

Do Pekys products use environmentally friendly materials?
Pekys Store is committed to continuously increasing the use of more environmentally friendly materials. Aerochill and ThermoWarm materials are designed with the principle of material efficiency that reduces the need for extra layers, thus indirectly reducing material consumption.
